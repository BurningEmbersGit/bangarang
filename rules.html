Rules

  1. Place the cards somewhere everyone can reach
  2. The most chaotic player goes first
  3. The active player picks up a card (if you don't like it, just take another) and reads out the questions one at a time
  4. The other players have to guess what the active player's answer to the question would be. If you've got an unfair advantage, guess last.
  5. The active player reveals their answer. Repeat this for the remaining questions on the card.
  6. Then the person to the active player's left becomes the active player.
  7. Scoring: If you want, keep track of how many guesses everyone gets right. The best guesser wins.
  8. The rules are made up! If you want, make up you're own (and if they're cool, tell me about it!)
